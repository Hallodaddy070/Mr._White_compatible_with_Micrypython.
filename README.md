# Mr.-White-Compatible-with-Micrypython-console-only-
Mr. White is a game where everyone gets a word and one person doesn't. After that everyone says a word relating to the word while the imposter tries not to get caught. Console only, compatible with Micropython. To play you choose the number of players and imposters, after you see your word you pass the calculator on to the next person. Made for fx-CG50.
